How to play Four in Line
4 in Line is a strategy game where two players take turns to place chips on a vertical board with 7 columns and 6 rows. Each player will have a chip color (which can be seen on their player cards). The game ends when one of the players manages to align four chips horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before their opponent, or when no more chips can be placed on the board.
